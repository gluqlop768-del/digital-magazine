Didactic guide

This section includes the main didactic information of the project. 

Here you can find: 

  • the sequence of tasks
  • the assessment criteria related to those tasks
  • and some methodological notes. 

Tasks and assessmet criteria

This sequence has been designed taking into account previous reflections on teaching practice, the need to use realistic digital tools and the importance of planning the project in a flexible and coherent way. 

Methodological notes

The project is designed for 2nd ESO students in the subject of English.
The final product is a digital magazine in English about linguistic diversity in Europe.
The project combines whole class work, pair work and small group work.
The main digital tools used are Padlet, Liveworksheets, Google Classroom and Canva.
The activities are organised in a progressive way, from comprehension and vocabulary work to the creation of the final product.
