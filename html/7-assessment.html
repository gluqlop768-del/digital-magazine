7. ASSESSMENT

Texto

Assessment in this project will be continuous, formative and varied. It will not only focus on the final digital magazine, but also on the whole learning process. The aim is to collect evidence of students’ progress, detect possible difficulties, offer personalised feedback and help students become more aware of their own learning. For this reason, different assessment tools will be used such as: a writing rubric and a self-assessment target diagram.
