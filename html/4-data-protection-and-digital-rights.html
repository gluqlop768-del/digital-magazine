4. DATA PROTECTION AND DIGITAL RIGHTS

Digital safety

During this project, we will use the internet and digital tools. For this reason, we must protect our personal data and respect everyone’s digital rights.

Basic rules:

  • Do not publish your full name, address, telephone number, personal email or any private information.
  • Use only your school account when working online.
  • Do not share your passwords.
  • Do not upload photos of yourself or your classmates without permission.
  • Do not publish other people’s work as if it were your own.
  • Use copyright-free images or images with Creative Commons licences.
  • Always cite the sources of the information, images, videos or audio files you use.
    Use respectful language in all digital spaces.
  • Before publishing any content, check it with your group and with your teacher.
  • Remember that everything you do online is part of your digital footprint.

With this project, we will learn how to use technology in a safe, critical, responsible and respectful way.
