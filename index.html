English in Europe: Designing an English Magazine to Promote Linguistic Diversity”.

Welcome to our project!

In this project, we are going to explore linguistic diversity in Europe and the role of English as a lingua franca in different areas such as education, tourism, culture and digital communication.

During the project, you will work in groups to create an informative digital magazine in English. To do this, you will search for reliable information, select relevant content, use digital tools and present your work orally to the rest of the class.

The main aim is to learn English in a practical, cooperative and meaningful way, while developing your digital competence and your respect for linguistic and cultural diversity.Image created by Guadalupe Luque
